Safety Guidelines

At Verity, your safety is our top priority. We are committed to creating a secure, respectful, and authentic environment for all our users. These guidelines are designed to help you have a positive experience and to understand our expectations for community behavior.

1. Respectful Conduct

  • Be Kind and Courteous: Treat everyone with respect, kindness, and empathy. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, or any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.
  • No Nudity or Explicit Content: Sharing or requesting explicit content, nudity, or sexually suggestive material is strictly prohibited. This includes in profile pictures, during Spark Calls, or in any communication within the app.
  • No Harassment or Aggression: Do not engage in any behavior that could be considered harassing, threatening, intimidating, or aggressive towards other users or our team.

2. Personal Information & Privacy

  • Protect Your Information: Never share sensitive personal information (e.g., home address, financial details, government ID numbers) with other users, especially during early interactions. Verity will never ask for your password.
  • Report Suspicious Activity: If another user asks for money, personal details, or engages in suspicious behavior, report them immediately.
  • Mutual Reveals: Your identity remains private during the initial Spark Call until a mutual reveal is agreed upon. Respect the privacy boundaries of others.

3. Authenticity & Verification

  • Be Yourself: Use your real identity and present yourself authentically. Misrepresentation, catfishing, or creating fake profiles is strictly forbidden.
  • Reporting Misrepresentation: If you suspect a profile is fake or a user is misrepresenting themselves, please report it.

4. During Spark Calls

  • Stay Appropriate: Maintain appropriate behavior and conversation during Spark Calls. Avoid any content that is offensive, illegal, or violates our terms.
  • No Recording: Do not record, screenshot, or share any part of a Spark Call without explicit consent from all participants.
  • Focus on Connection: Use the 45-second Spark Call to genuinely connect and discover chemistry, not to solicit or engage in inappropriate behavior.

5. Reporting & Moderation

  • Easy Reporting: Verity provides easy-to-use in-app reporting tools. If you experience or witness any behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it.
  • Real-time Moderation: Our platform employs advanced AI and human moderators to actively monitor Spark Calls and interactions for inappropriate content or behavior.
  • Consequences of Violations: Violations of these guidelines will result in swift action, including warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent account bans.

6. External Interactions

  • Be Cautious: If you decide to meet someone in person after connecting on Verity, always exercise caution. Meet in a public place, inform a friend or family member of your plans, and arrange your own transportation.
  • Verity Does Not Screen Users: While we have moderation, Verity does not conduct background checks on users. It is your responsibility to make safe choices.
