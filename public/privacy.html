Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-26

At Verity, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with the Australian Privacy Principles.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details (email, phone number hash)
  • Profile information (display name, gender)
  • Biometric data (selfie verification analysis) processed transiently and not permanently stored for user discovery.

2. Live Video and Moderation

Video calls are monitored by automated AI moderation for policy violations. We do not record video calls by default. Frames are processed transiently and discarded unless a severe violation requires review.

3. How We Use Data

We use collected data to operate, secure, and improve Verity, including matchmaking and safety enforcement.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share only required data with vetted providers (for example payments and real-time infrastructure) to run the service.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. You can delete your account from app settings.
